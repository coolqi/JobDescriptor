Organisational Details

ABC Corp is a leading Australian telecommunications services company providing low cost mobile connectivity across all states and territories. On the back of strong revenue growth, ABC Corp is now investing heavily in multiple data related technologies including broadband internet, Voice over IP and state of the art data centres supporting small businesses.In FY 15, ABC Corp has revenue of $200 million, NPAT of $28 million and services more than 500,000 customers. ABC Corp employs 2,000 staff and has offices in all major cities across Australia.

Business Unit Details

The Finance Business Unit, led by the Chief Financial Officer, is responsible for all procurement, financial analytics, investor relations and financial control. The treasury and company secretary also belong to Finance. The BU has 75 full time employees and works very closely with Risk, HR and IT.

IT is a major focus for ABC Corp, given its strategic priority of investing heavily in data related technologies. The Chief Information Officer provides leadership across Data, IT architecture, Business Solutions and Cyber Security. There are more than 200 full time and 35 part time employees working in IT.

Team Details

The Financial Control is led by the Financial Controller. Reporting to the Financial Controller are Finance Operations Manager and Management Accounting Manager. Revenue and Expenditure analysts report to Finance Operations Manager while management accountants report to Management Accounting Manager. This team is responsible for ensuring internal and external controls are in place as well as managing entire financial planning activities.

The Head of Analytics lead the Financial Analytics team within Finance. Business Analysts, Analytics Manager and other Reporting Analysts report to the Head of Analytics. Financial Analytics is accountable for reporting on ABC Corp’s financial performance and employee numbers such as absenteeism and leaves taken.

The General Manager, Data is responsible for harnessing power of big data including ensuring its security. The GM is supported by data analysts and consultants.

Business Solutions take care of all solutions delivery aspect of the products and software including testing, automation, verification and maintenance. The General Manager has direct reports in automation and testing manager and technology change manager who in turn have solution and testing engineers and change and business analysts reporting to them.
